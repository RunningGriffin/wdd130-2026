Overview

Purpose

The Purpose of the site is to bring attention to the Competetive Ultimate Frisbee Scene in Rexburg, ID. It will showcase photos of the players during tournaments to build credability and also have a way to find out how to join.

Audience

There is 2 main groups targetted with one being the main focus. The first, and more cruical, is that of young adults in the Rexburg, Idaho area that are looking for a high level sport at a college that lacks more than intramural play. The second is lovers of Ultimate Frisbee that wish to spectate and "keep up with" their local Ultimate team.

Content

Home page

Welcome to the home page for the preimere Ultimate Frisbee Club team in all of eastern Idaho, T-Rexburg! We are a competetive travel club team that mostly plays in Idaho and Utah against club, college, and pro level competition. With a team that's been around for 14 years already, we aren't going to stop training and put eastern Idaho on the map as a Ultimate Frisbee Hub. Find out how to join us by contacting us here!

Who are we looking for to join our team? Ideally we look for anyone between the ages of 18-32 within a hour drive from Rexburg, Idaho. Above all else tho, we want players that have a drive and passion for the sport. No matter your skill, don't count yourself out! We have plenty of rookies that are still learning the basics of the sport and would love to teach new players how to play Ultimate Frisbee at a high level.

What is Ultimate Frisbee?

Ultimate Frisbee is a 7 on 7 non-contact sport played on a feild 70 yards long and 40 years wide with enzones running 20 yards deep on either end. each point begins with a PULL which can be compared to a kickoff in football. The 2 teams of 7 start at opposite endzones and one team throws off to the other to begin play. The receiveing team begins the point on offense with the onjective of passing the disc down the feild from teammate to teammate until it is caught in the endzone. Players can't run with the disc and have to establish a pivot foot after catching it. Anytime the disc is intercepted, incomplete, dropped, or caught out of bounds it is a turn-over. And the defense become the offense without any stop in play. For more in-depth rules at higher level of play, refer to the official rulebook https://usaultimate.org/rules/ or contact us!

Do you want to be taught how to play Ultimate Frisbee in person? T-Rexburg is a team local to the Rexburg, Idaho area with open practices for anyone to join. We also host casual pickups if you want a more subtle way to be introduced into the sport. Please feel free to contact us to get any information about these opportunities to learn the sport we dedicate ourselves to every week.
