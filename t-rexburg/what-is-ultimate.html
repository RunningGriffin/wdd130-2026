What is Ultimate?

Ultimate Frisbee is a 7 on 7 non-contact sport played on a field 70 yards long and 40 yards wide with endzones running 20 yards deep on either end. Each point begins with a PULL which can be compared to a kickoff in football. The 2 teams of 7 start at opposite endzones and one team throws off to the other to begin play.

The receiving team begins the point on offense with the objective of passing the disc down the field from teammate to teammate until it is caught in the endzone. Players can't run with the disc and have to establish a pivot foot after catching it. Anytime the disc is intercepted, incomplete, dropped, or caught out of bounds it is a turnover — and the defense becomes the offense without any stop in play.

For more in-depth rules, refer to the official rulebook at usaultimate.org/rules or contact us!

Spirit of the Game

One of the most unique aspects of Ultimate is that it is self-refereed, even at high levels of competition. Players are responsible for their own foul calls and resolving disputes, guided by a strong tradition of fair play known as "Spirit of the Game."

Why Play?

Ultimate is a great workout, a welcoming community, and a sport that anyone can learn regardless of prior athletic experience. Come out and give it a try — we'd love to have you!
